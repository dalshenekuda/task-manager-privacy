Privacy Policy

Task Dependencies Manager for Monday.com

Last Updated: November 16, 2024

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. The app works directly with Monday.com's API and all your data stays in your Monday.com workspace.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Task Dependencies Manager ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our Monday.com application.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or user information.

The App operates entirely within your Monday.com workspace and:

  • Does not send data to any external servers
  • Does not store data in any database
  • Does not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Does not collect analytics or usage statistics
  • Does not require user registration

3. How the App Works

The App functions as follows:

  • Direct API Access: The App reads task dependency information directly from your Monday.com board using Monday.com's official API
  • Real-time Display: Data is displayed in real-time and is never stored or cached by us
  • Client-side Only: All processing happens in your browser, not on our servers

4. Data Accessed from Monday.com

When you use the App, it accesses the following information from your Monday.com workspace through the official Monday.com API:

  • Task names
  • Task statuses
  • Due dates
  • Task dependencies (predecessor and dependent relationships)

Important: This data is only read for display purposes and is never stored, transmitted to third parties, or used for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data, there is no data to secure on our end. All your data remains securely within your Monday.com workspace, protected by Monday.com's security measures.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with or share data with any third-party services except Monday.com's official API, which is required for the App to function.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any personal data:

  • There is no data to request access to
  • There is no data to delete
  • There is no data to modify

All your data remains in your Monday.com workspace, where you have full control over it according to Monday.com's terms and policies.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Compliance

This App complies with:

  • Monday.com's App Marketplace policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

11. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us through the Monday.com App Marketplace.
